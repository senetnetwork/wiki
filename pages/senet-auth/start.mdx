## What is Senet Auth?

Senet Auth has adopted the single sign-on to lower the threshold for Internet users to enter web3.0. It enables free, seamless, one-click authentication. At the same time, we plan to use MPC decentralized private keys can make more secure private keys distributed in independent blockchain networks in the future.

## What are the features of Senet Auth?

- **Free & Fast** - Uses the common Internet login method to  speed up your steps into web3.0 and we provide this service free of charge.
- **Go mainstream** - Supports mainstream social authentication providers that users are familiar with (Google, Facebook, Twitter, Discord, Apple, etc.)
- **Senet-Chain support** - Equips with senet's own game blockchain, making the experience more smooth.
- **Web3 wallet support** - Want to use MetaMask or Others to authenticate? No problem! You can still authenticate with mainstream web3 wallets on Senet Auth.
